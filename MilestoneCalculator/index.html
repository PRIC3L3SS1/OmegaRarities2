How to Use This Tool

Before You Start

  1. Go to Settings and disable Rune Luck.
  2. Set your rune to Slime Rune — the rare rune you're farming for is Genesis Slime. This method works for most runes, but Slime is the go-to.
  3. Go to your Profile and find your Rune Bulk amount. This is your starting bulk.
  4. Enter your starting bulk and your current rare rune milestone tier into the tool.

The Search Process

We're looking for the exact bulk that produces runes at 1/50,000. This tool uses binary search to find it efficiently.

  1. Click Start Search. The tool will give you a bulk amount to test.
  2. Copy the bulk value and change your bulk amount to it.
  3. Try farming runes for a bit, then report the result:
    • Too Low — You couldn't get the rare rune at all after a minute. Bulk is too small.
    • Too High — You got the rare rune, but it didn't count toward your milestone. Bulk is too large.
    • Perfect — You got the rare rune AND it counted toward your milestone! This is the sweet spot.
  4. Repeat until you find the perfect bulk. A "perfect" bulk means you're getting a rare rune counted towards your milestone every 30-40 seconds.
Tip: After hitting "Perfect", you can click "Refine" to narrow it down even further if you want extra precision. The first "Perfect" is usually good enough though.

Tier Projections

Once you find your perfect bulk, the tool automatically calculates what your bulk should be for the upcoming tiers. Each milestone tier-up increases your rune speed by 1.13x, so your bulk needs to decrease by the same factor to stay at the sweet spot.

Returning user? If you already know your bulk, use the "Returning User" section. Enter your known bulk and tier to see the projection table.
