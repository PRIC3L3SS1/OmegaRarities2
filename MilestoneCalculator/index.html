How to Use This Tool

What You Need

  1. Go to Settings and disable Rune Luck.
  2. Enter your RPS (visible on screen), your current rare rune milestone tier, your Rune Bulk (from your Profile), and your Rune Clone count.
  3. Set your bulk to the amount it shows, and farm the rune it says to farm. (e.g., Genesis Slime)

Tier Projections

Each rare rune milestone tier increases your Rune Speed by 1.13×. The projections table shows what your perfect bulk will be at each future tier, accounting for the speed increase. You should update your bulk after tiering up if your rare rune milestone is no longer increasing.
